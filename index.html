Updating styles with Sass is easy!

Every color on this page is calculated off of one global variable.

You can change all of the background and font colors by editing just that one variable.

Sass also allows you to nest your declarations, create custom functions, extend declarations to selectors, use partials, and create mixins.

Hover effects on this page are nested under their parent elements, and the left nav bar uses a function to set the even numbered links to a different color.
